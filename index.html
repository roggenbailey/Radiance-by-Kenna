Meet Kenna–Licensed Esthetician

I’ve been passionate about skincare and helping others feel confident in their own skin. What started as a personal love for all things beauty and wellness turned into a fulfilling career when I became a licensed esthetician. I truly believe that radiant skin is more than just appearance—it’s about self-care, confidence, and feeling your best from the inside out.

My goal is to create a relaxing, judgment-free space where you can unwind while receiving treatments that are tailored to your unique skin needs. Whether you're looking to treat acne, boost hydration, smooth fine lines, or just enjoy a rejuvenating facial, I’m here to help you on your skincare journey.

I also offer a variety of lash and brow services to enhance your natural beauty. Every treatment I provide is done with intention, care, and a focus on results.
